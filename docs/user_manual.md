# KiTN POS — Comprehensive User Manual

Welcome to the KiTN POS training guide. This document provides step-by-step instructions for owners and staff on how to use every feature of the platform effectively.

---

## 🏠 Dashboard: Store at a Glance
The Dashboard is your primary mission control center.

- **KPI Cards**: Monitor "Today's Sales," "Transactions," and "Daily Average" in real-time.
- **Low Stock Alerts**: If an item hits its reorder level, it will appear here with a red alert. Click it to go directly to restocking.
- **Recent Receipts**: Quick access to the last 10 sales for verification or re-printing.

---

## 🛒 POS Terminal: Selling & Checkout
This is where your staff will spend 90% of their time.

- **Adding Products**: Use the search bar or physical barcode scanner to add items to the cart.
- **Quantity Adjustments**: Click the `+` or `-` buttons next to any item in the cart.
- **Checkout Process**:
  1. Click the green "Checkout" button.
  2. Select Customer Payment Method: **M-Pesa**, **Cash**, or **Card**.
  3. Enter "Cash Tendered" for change calculation.
  4. Confirm and print the receipt.
- **Receipts**: After every sale, a digital receipt appears. Use the "Print" button for physical copies.

---

## 📦 Inventory Management: Keeping Stock
Proper inventory tracking is the backbone of your store.

- **Restocking**: When new stock arrives, find the item and click "Restock." Enter the quantity and cost per unit.
- **Stock Adjustment**: If items are damaged or stolen, use "Adjustment" to manually correct the count. Select a reason (e.g., "Theft," "Expiry").
- **Low Stock Warnings**: Items highlighted in **orange** are near empty. Items in **red** are out of stock.

---

## 📊 Sales Reports: Analytics
Use these reports to grow your business.

- **Filtering**: View sales for "Today," "This Week," or "This Month."
- **Charts**: Identify your peak sales days at a glance.
- **Data Export**: Click "Export CSV" to download all transaction data for external bookkeeping.
- **Duplicate Receipts**: Find any past sale in the transaction log and click "View" to see the original receipt.

---

## 💰 Finance: P&L & Expenses
Track your money, not just your sales.

- **Revenue**: Automatically synced from the POS Terminal.
- **Expenses**: Manually log costs such as "Rent," "Electricity," or "Supplier Payments."
- **Net Profit**: The system calculates your true profit by subtracting expenses from net sales.
- **Upgrade Prompts**: Advanced financial analytics require a premium subscription.

---

## 👥 Team & Performance
Manage your staff members.

- **Shift Clock**: Staff must click "Clock In Now" at the start of their shift. They must "Clock Out" before leaving.
- **Leaderboard**: See who is making the most sales today to identify top performers.
- **Activity Log**: View exactly what each staff member did during their shift.

---

## 🏪 Outlets & Branches
Manage multiple locations from one login.

- **Branch Selection**: The dropdown at the top of the sidebar allows switching between outlets (Owners only).
- **Stock Transfers**: Send inventory from the "Main Warehouse" to a "Retail Branch." The system handles the subtraction and addition automatically.

---

## ⚙️ Settings: Customization
- **Business Profile**: Update your store name, logo (for receipts), and address.
- **User Management**: Add new staff members and assign their roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier).
- **Subscription**: Manage your KiTN plan and view billing history.
