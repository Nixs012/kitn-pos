# KiTN POS — User Onboarding & Training Guide

This guide is for business owners and trainers to use when teaching new staff how to operate KiTN POS. Use the following structured steps to ensure every employee is fully onboarded.

---

## 📅 Part 1: First-Time Setup (Owner/Admin)
Only admins should perform these steps once.

1. **Onboarding Wizard**: Complete Business Details, Branch Setup, and First Inventory.
2. **Subscription Selection**: Choose the Basic or Premium plan to unlock advanced features like Multi-branch.
3. **User Creation**: 
   - Go to **Settings > Users**.
   - Invite your staff using their email.
   - Assign the `Cashier` role to restrict their access to Finance and Settings.

---

## 💳 Part 2: Training Your Cashiers
Follow this checklist when training a new cashier:

### 1. The Clock-In Ritual
- Teach the staff to clock in immediately upon arrival via the **Team** page.
- Explain that their sales performance is tracked from this moment.

### 2. POS Terminal Mastery
- Show how to scan items or search for products using the search bar.
- Practise a **M-Pesa Checkout**: Enter the M-Pesa transaction code once confirmed.
- Practise a **Cash Checkout**: Show how the system calculates the "Change Due."
- **Exercise**: Have the cashier process 3 test sales and print 3 test receipts.

### 3. Inventory Awareness
- Show the cashier where the "Low Stock" alerts appear on the Dashboard.
- Explain that if an item is out of stock, it will be disabled in the POS Cart.

---

## 📉 Part 3: Training Your Managers
Managers need to understand data and stock control.

### 1. Restocking Flow
- Teach how to find a product in the **Inventory** list.
- Show how to add quantity and unit cost.
- Explain the importance of accurate `Reorder Levels` to prevent stock-outs.

### 2. Basic Troubleshooting
- If the barcode scanner is not working: Check the physical connection or browser permissions for the camera.
- If a receipt is lost: Navigate to **Reports > Sales** to find the transaction and reprint.

---

## 🛠️ Training Checklist for All Users
- [ ] Log in with your email/password.
- [ ] Clock in via the Team page.
- [ ] Complete a sale with 3+ items.
- [ ] Handle an M-Pesa payment correctly.
- [ ] View the daily sales totals on the Dashboard.
- [ ] Clock out at the end of the shift.

---

## 💡 Top Training Tips
- **Real Data**: Let staff practice with real items, but delete the test sales via the **Database Reset** tool (Admin only) if needed before going live.
- **Mobile First**: If they are using a smartphone, show them the **Bottom Tab Bar** for navigation.
- **Feedback**: Encourage staff to watch the "Low Stock" alert—it's the most critical warning for the store's health.
